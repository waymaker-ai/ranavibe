# Press Release: RANA Launch

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

---

## Waymaker Launches RANA: Open-Source Framework Reduces AI Development Costs by 70%

### Universal LLM Integration Platform Saves Companies $441,000 Annually While Eliminating Vendor Lock-In

**[CITY, STATE] - January XX, 2025** - Waymaker today announced the launch of RANA (Rapid AI Native Architecture), an open-source framework that unifies nine major Large Language Model (LLM) providers behind a single API. Early adopters report 70% cost reductions and 120x faster development times.

---

## THE PROBLEM

Modern AI application development faces critical fragmentation. Each LLM provider—OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and others—maintains unique APIs, forcing developers to choose between vendor lock-in or spending 40+ hours per provider integration. Industry analysis suggests this fragmentation costs the global developer community $18 billion annually in lost productivity.

---

## THE SOLUTION

RANA provides a unified abstraction layer that supports nine LLM providers through one API. Developers can switch providers with a single line of code while benefiting from automatic cost optimization, enterprise-grade security, and production-ready infrastructure.

"We rebuilt the same LLM integration for every client," said Ashley Kays, Co-founder of Waymaker and creator of RANA. "After the fifth time, we knew there had to be a better way. RANA is that better way."

---

## PROVEN RESULTS

Five companies across e-commerce, SaaS, and healthcare sectors have deployed RANA in production with measurable results:

- **E-commerce customer support:** Reduced costs from $22,500 to $6,750 monthly (70% reduction)
- **SaaS content generation:** Saved $151,200 annually while improving output quality 25%
- **Healthcare AI assistant:** Cut compliance audit preparation from 100 hours to 10 hours

**Combined annual savings across early adopters: $441,000**

---

## TECHNICAL INNOVATION

RANA achieves cost reduction through four mechanisms:

1. **Smart Model Selection:** Automatically routes simple tasks to cost-effective providers (Gemini Flash at $0.10/1M tokens) and complex tasks to premium providers (Claude/GPT-4 at $3-5/1M tokens)

2. **Response Caching:** Serves identical queries from Redis in under 10ms, achieving 40% cache hit rates

3. **Prompt Optimization:** Eliminates wasted tokens through template reuse and automatic optimization

4. **RAG Implementation:** Reduces context window sizes by 20x through vector search integration

---

## INDUSTRY SUPPORT

RANA supports nine LLM providers:
- OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini)
- Anthropic (Claude 3.5 Sonnet/Haiku)
- Google (Gemini 2.0 Flash/Pro)
- xAI (Grok Beta)
- Mistral AI
- Cohere
- Together.ai
- Groq
- Ollama (local deployment)

---

## OPEN SOURCE COMMITMENT

RANA is released under the MIT license with no usage restrictions, pricing tiers, or enterprise limitations.

"We want RANA to become the industry standard," said Christian Moore, Co-founder and technical architect. "React changed how we build UIs. We believe RANA will change how we build AI applications."

---

## AVAILABILITY

RANA v2.0 is available immediately at https://rana.dev

- **Framework:** MIT licensed, free forever
- **Languages:** TypeScript, Python (Go SDK Q1 2025)
- **Deployment:** Compatible with Vercel, Railway, AWS, GCP, Azure
- **Support:** Community Discord, comprehensive documentation
