## ‚úÖ Day 6-7: Discovery Calls (As booked)

### Call Structure (30 minutes)

**Minutes 0-5: Introduction**
- "Thanks for taking the time"
- "Tell me about what you're building"
- Listen more than talk

**Minutes 5-15: Discovery**
Ask:
1. What are you trying to build?
2. What's your timeline? (funding deadline?)
3. What's your budget range?
4. Have you worked with agencies before?
5. What does success look like in 3 months?

**Minutes 15-25: Present Solution**
- "Based on what you said..."
- Explain RANA approach
- Show Bettr as example
- Discuss timeline (realistic)

**Minutes 25-30: Next Steps**
- "Does this approach make sense?"
- "What questions do you have?"
- "I'll send a proposal in 24 hours"
- Book follow-up if needed

### After the Call
1. Send thank you email (within 1 hour)
2. Draft proposal (same day)
3. Send proposal (within 24 hours)
4. Follow up in 48-72 hours

## üí¨ If You Get Stuck

**No responses to outreach?**
- Make it more personal (less template-y)
- Add specific value for their company
- Follow up 2-3 times

**No discovery calls booking?**
- Offer specific times (not just "let's chat")
- Use Calendly to remove friction
- Follow up via DM if they showed interest

**Discovery calls not converting?**
- Listen more, talk less
- Focus on their pain points
- Don't sell, just help them see solution
- Send proposal quickly (same day)

**Proposals not closing?**
- Follow up! Most deals close after 3-5 touches
- Address concerns directly
- Offer to jump on a quick call
- Create urgency (founding client discount expires)
