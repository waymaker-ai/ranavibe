## üí° Pro Tips

### Getting the Best Results:

1. **Be Specific:** The more details you provide, the better
2. **Iterate:** Generate 10-20 variations, pick the best
3. **Test at Scale:** View at 16x16px to ensure it works small
4. **Keep it Simple:** Fewer details = more memorable
5. **Think Silhouette:** Should be recognizable in pure black
6. **Consider Emotions:** Plan for 5-7 expressions minimum
7. **Brand Consistency:** Use exact hex codes for colors

### Testing Checklist:

- [ ] Works at 16x16px (favicon size)
- [ ] Recognizable in pure black silhouette
- [ ] Friendly, not scary
- [ ] Memorable after 3-second glance
- [ ] Different from competitors
- [ ] Scales to large sizes (billboard)
- [ ] Works on light and dark backgrounds
- [ ] Emotionally resonates
- [ ] Matches brand personality
- [ ] Easy to reproduce (not too complex)
