## Frequently Asked Questions

### "Should I offer all services from day 1?"
**No.** Start with finishing services (AI Prototype Finishing, Cost Rescue). Once you have 10+ clients and case studies, add premium services.

### "Can I do both finishing and custom builds?"
**Yes, but not day 1.** Focus on finishing first (easier to sell, faster delivery, higher margin). Add custom builds in Month 3-6.

### "What if a finishing client needs custom development?"
**Upsell them.** Start with finishing ($4,900, 7 days), then offer Feature Sprint ($8,000, 2 weeks) or full Custom AI App ($25K+).

### "How do I decide which service to recommend?"
**Ask these questions:**
1. Do they have existing code? (Yes = Finishing, No = Custom)
2. What's their budget? (<$5K = Finishing, >$25K = Custom)
3. What's their timeline? (<2 weeks = Finishing, >4 weeks = Custom)
4. What's their main pain? (Code quality = Finishing, No code = Custom, Costs = Cost Rescue)

### "What's the highest LTV customer path?"
**Path:** AI Prototype Finishing ‚Üí AI Cost Rescue ‚Üí Feature Sprints ‚Üí Digital Marketing ‚Üí Managed Hosting
**LTV:** $4,900 + $2,900 + $8,000 + $9,000 + $4,500 = $29,300 (first 6 months)
